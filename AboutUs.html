    About Us

    Welcome to Tea Creme, your one-stop
    shop for the best milk tea in town! We offer
    a wide variety of milk tea flavors, including
    classics like taro and boba, as well as more
    unique flavors like our Cheesecake
    and Oreo series!

    We also offer a variety of other beverages,
    including Lemonades and Frappe Series.

    What's Our Story

    Our journey as Tea Creme began in 2020 as a simple milk tea shop dedicated to crafting delightful drinks for those who like to crave sweet, refreshing, and trendy cold beverages. In 2021, under the same ownership, we collaborated with our sister store ‘Kebab n’ Wrap’—“May pambara na, may panulak pa!”, to serve perfect combinations of delicious foods and drinks hand in hand in H. Santos St, Brgy. Tejeros, Makati.

    However, due to the challenges posed by the pandemic, we temporarily closed our doors in a responsible effort to curb the spread of COVID-19. But then again, we bounced back with resilience, reopening after a few weeks and unveiling new branches in Las Pinas and BGC, ensuring the continuation of serving the creamiest milk tea in town!

    Indeed, a cup of Tea Creme shared with friends is a good memory that never ends!
